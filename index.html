Hi, Yih-Shiuan (易萱 a.k.a. 小黑) here! Welcome to my personal website! I'm glad you found me here!

As a visual neuroscientist, I use behavioral and neuroscience techniques to study human vision (i.e. how we see and understand the world).

I come from Taiwan and received my PhD from the lab of Prof. Mark Greenlee in University of Regensburg, Germany.

Now I'm working as a Postdoc in Mark's lab. As our project is approaching to its end, I'm actively searching for my next position.

If you know anyone searching for a visual neuroscientist with expertise in psychophysics, fMRI, and MEG, let me know!

You will find out more about my research and some very cool visual illusioin demos on this site.

Enjoy it! Feel free to contact me if you're interested in knowing more about me and/or my work.
